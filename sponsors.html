Top 2 Brands That Sponsor MTB:

Redbull

Redbull is known as the most prestigous sponsor that can be acquired, it is what most pros aim for.

Being sponsored by Redbull comes with

  • Extensive financial support
  • High-level media production
  • Access to top tier events with exclusive access

Monster

Monster sponsors different kinds of riding, such as skatepark and freeride.

Monster offers:

  • Discounts on gear
  • Provided mentorship
  • Access to more events
