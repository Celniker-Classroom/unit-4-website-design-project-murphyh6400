Popular bike parks:

Whistler Bike Park

Click for a click to Whistler's page

Whistler is known as the overall best bike park, with famous trails such as:

  • A-line
  • Dirt Merchant
  • Crank-it-up

If pre-booked, whistler costs around $75 USD per day for an adult, making it very affordable and an insanely good park.

Leogang Bike Park

Cick for a link to Leogang's page

Leogang is popular for their very difficult trails, some where speeds of 40MPH+ are reached. Leogang is a great bike park for people of the skill level intermediate to pro.

Popular trails include:

  • Hot Shots
  • Flying Gangster
  • Hangman I/II

Leogang is located in Austria, and costs around $38 USD for a full day ticket.

Luiseno Bike Park

Click for a link to Luiseno's page

Luiseno is a more local bike park, around 60 minutes from Carmel Valley.

Main trails there include:

  • Granite Falls, technical downhill trail
  • Stonehenge, jump trail
  • Acorn, flow jump trail

Luiseno costs around $50 USD for an adult per day, but they offer year passes for $500.
