Popular Brands:

1. Specialized Bikes

This is a link to Specialized.

Specialized Prioritizes Three Main Things in Their Designs:

  • Riding speed
  • Experience
  • Durability

Specialized is most well known for their Tarmac bike, used for road racing, their Stumpjumper, used for trails and dirt, and their Allez, for more entry-level road biking.

2. Commencal

This is a link to Commencal

Commencal is Known for a Few Things:

  • Being a no-carbon brand. Commencal prioritizes high quality alluminum for more durability downhill racing, and also environmental preservation.
  • The founder of Commencal implemented a fun based philosophy, meaning they want every bike to be capable and fun to ride.
  • Finally, Commencal thinks it is important for all ages to enjoy riding, so they make high quality children's downhill, cross country, and dirt jump bikes.

Commencal is mostly known for their Meta SX V5 and their Clash Freeride.

3. Pivot Cycles

This is a link to Pivot.

Pivot Prides Themselves For:

  • High performance suspension
  • Pedalling efficiency (anti-squat)
  • Stiff, precise, and rigid feel

Pivot is known for their Switchblade trail bike, their Firebird enduro bike, and the Mach 4 SL cross country racer.
